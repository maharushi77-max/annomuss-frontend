🔒 Why This Data Is Genuine — Answer for Investors & Advertisers
1️⃣
Users chose their emotions voluntarily. Every emotion tag was selected by the user when posting. No algorithm guessed — they said "I'm feeling anxious" by selecting it themselves.
Self-declared
2️⃣
Multiple independent signals confirm each other. If a user tags "anxious" AND saves an "anxious" post AND reacts "heard" to another anxious post, that's 3 independent data points all pointing the same direction. Confidence score rises.
Triangulated
3️⃣
Behavioral signals cross-validate text signals. We compare what users say (post emotion tag) with what they do (what posts they stop to like or save). When both match → high confidence. When they don't match → we flag the discrepancy.
Cross-validated
4️⃣
This is the only platform where users came specifically to share emotions. Unlike Instagram or YouTube where users come for entertainment, Annomuss users open the app TO process how they feel. Their emotional expression is the entire purpose of their visit — not a side effect.
Context-native
5️⃣
Anonymous = more honest. Research shows people are significantly more truthful in anonymous settings. Users express emotions they would never post on Instagram. This data captures their real internal state, not their social performance.
Anonymity effect
6️⃣
Timestamps create a verifiable audit trail. Every signal has a timestamp, user ID, and source reference. Advertisers can audit which signals led to which segment classification at any time. No black box.
Auditable
