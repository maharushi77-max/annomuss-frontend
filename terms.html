Welcome to Annomuss

By using Annomuss (annomuss.in), you agree to these terms. Annomuss is an anonymous social platform where you can express yourself freely and safely.

Who can use Annomuss

  • You must be 13 years or older to use Annomuss
  • You need a valid email address to create an account
  • One account per person

What you can do

  • ✅ Share honestly. Be vulnerable. Express yourself.
  • ✅ Support others. Leave kind reactions. Send compliments.
  • ✅ Use random chat to connect with people anonymously.
  • ✅ Post confessions, vents, unsent letters, and stories.

What you cannot do

  • ❌ Harass, bully, or target any person
  • ❌ Share anyone's personal information (doxxing)
  • ❌ Post explicit, violent, or harmful content
  • ❌ Use this platform to harm yourself or others
  • ❌ Create multiple accounts or fake identities
  • ❌ Spam, scam, or use bots

Your content

You own what you post. By posting, you give Annomuss permission to display it on the platform. Posts are anonymous — we don't attach your name or email to public content.

Account removal

We may remove your account if you violate these terms. You can delete your account anytime in Settings → Security & Privacy → Delete Account.

Changes to terms

We may update these terms. Continued use of Annomuss means you accept the updated terms.

🔒 Your privacy matters

What we collect

  • Email address — for login via OTP only. Never shown publicly.
  • Anonymous name — chosen by you. Shown on posts.
  • Mood logs — emotion + city, stored for 90 days, used to personalise your feed.
  • Posts and messages — your content stored securely.
  • Location (city-level only) — used for emotion pulse feature. Never exact GPS.

What we do NOT collect

  • ❌ Your real name
  • ❌ Phone number
  • ❌ Exact GPS location
  • ❌ Device contacts
  • ❌ Browsing history

How we use your data

  • To log you in securely via OTP
  • To show you a personalised feed based on your emotions
  • To send push notifications when someone reacts to your post
  • To detect and remove harmful content

Who we share data with

We do not sell your data. We use the following services:

  • MongoDB Atlas — database hosting
  • Cloudinary — image and video storage
  • Resend — OTP email delivery
  • Render — backend hosting
  • Vercel — frontend hosting

Data retention

  • OTPs: deleted after 10 minutes
  • Stories: deleted after 24 hours
  • Mood logs: deleted after 90 days
  • Account data: kept until you delete your account

Your rights (DPDP Act 2023)

Under India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act 2023, you have the right to access, correct, and delete your personal data. Email us at support@annomuss.in to exercise these rights.

Cookies

We use localStorage (not cookies) to store your login token and preferences on your device. No third-party tracking cookies.

🛡️ Your safety is our priority

Anonymous but not unaccountable

Annomuss is anonymous — but we take safety seriously. We have systems to detect harmful content and we act on reports quickly.

Crisis detection

We automatically detect posts that may indicate someone is in crisis. If your post is flagged, you'll see a gentle message with support resources. You are not alone.

How to report

  • Tap ⋯ on any post → Report post
  • Tap ⋯ in any DM → Report user
  • Email: support@annomuss.in

Blocking

You can block any user. Blocked users cannot message you or appear in your feed. Go to ⋯ on any post or in messages to block.
