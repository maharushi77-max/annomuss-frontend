You must be at least 13 years old to use Annomuss. By using this platform, you confirm you meet this age requirement. If you are under 18, you must have permission from a parent or guardian.

What Annomuss is

Annomuss is an anonymous social platform where users share confessions, vents, unsent letters, and connect through random chat. We are committed to keeping the platform safe, honest, and supportive.

Anonymous, not invisible. While your posts are anonymous, Annomuss maintains internal logs for safety and legal compliance. True anonymity cannot be guaranteed if illegal activity is reported to authorities.

🔒 Privacy Policy

Your Data on Annomuss

We take your privacy seriously. This explains what we collect, why, and your rights under the DPDP Act 2023.
Last updated: March 2026 · DPDP Act 2023 compliant

What we collect

📧 Account data
Your email address (used only for OTP login), your anonymous name (randomly assigned), and your chosen avatar emoji.
📝 Content data
Posts, confessions, vents, unsent letters, and comments you create. These are stored anonymously — not linked to your real identity in any public-facing way.
📍 Location data
Your city/state (if you allow location access) — used only for emotion pulse map and local matching. We never store your precise GPS coordinates.
⚡ Usage data
XP earned, mood logs, streak data, and basic activity (posts, chats) — used to power your profile stats and badges. Never sold or shared.

What we do NOT collect

  • Your real name, phone number, or physical address
  • Payment information (we don't charge you anything)
  • Your contacts, photos outside of posts, or device sensors
  • Your browsing history outside of Annomuss

How we use your data

  • To provide and improve the platform
  • To match you in random chat by emotion and location
  • To show you relevant content in the feed
  • To detect crisis content and surface helplines
  • To send you OTP verification emails (nothing else)
We do not sell your data. We do not have ads. We do not share your data with third parties for marketing purposes. Your data exists only to make Annomuss work for you.

Data retention

Your account data is kept until you delete your account. Posts and vents remain anonymous on the platform after deletion (they cannot be linked back to you). OTP codes expire in 10 minutes. Mood data is kept for 90 days then auto-deleted.

Your rights (DPDP Act 2023)

  • Right to access — you can request a copy of your data at any time
  • Right to correction — you can update your profile settings at any time
  • Right to erasure — you can delete your account from Settings → Security → Delete Account
  • Right to withdraw consent — you can opt out of location access in your device settings
  • Right to grievance redressal — email support@annomuss.in within 72 hours for a response

Cookies and storage

We use localStorage (browser storage) to keep you logged in and save your preferences. We do not use third-party tracking cookies. No cookie consent banner needed — we keep it clean.

Third-party services

  • Cloudinary — stores images and videos you upload (their privacy policy applies)
  • Resend — sends your OTP emails (they only see your email address)
  • MongoDB Atlas — hosts our database securely on AWS servers
  • Render.com — hosts our backend server
  • Vercel — hosts our frontend

Children's privacy

Annomuss is not directed at children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 is using our platform, please contact us and we will remove their account promptly.

Contact & Grievance Officer

For privacy concerns or data requests, contact our Grievance Officer at privacy@annomuss.in. We will respond within 72 hours as required by the DPDP Act 2023.

🛡️ Community Safety

Keeping Annomuss Safe

We care deeply about the mental health and safety of everyone on Annomuss. Here's how we protect our community.

Crisis detection

Annomuss uses AI to detect signs of distress in posts and vents. If our system detects a potential crisis, we surface mental health resources and helpline numbers immediately. This feature exists because we believe one conversation can save a life.

💙 If you're in crisis right now

You are not alone. Please reach out to one of these services:

📞 iCall India — 9152987821 📞 Vandrevala Foundation — 1860-2662-345

How to report

Every post has a ⋯ menu with a "Report" option. Reports go directly to our moderation team. We review every report within 24 hours and take action where needed.

Content moderation

  • All posts go through AI safety screening before being published
  • Content flagged with high confidence is automatically hidden pending review
  • Users who repeatedly violate community guidelines are banned
  • Admin dashboard allows our team to review and act on reports

Anonymous, not consequence-free

Being anonymous doesn't mean you can harm others. Harassment, threats, and abuse are taken seriously. We cooperate with law enforcement when required by law. Anonymity protects your identity, not your behaviour.

Blocking and muting

You can block any user from the Social → Discover page. Blocked users cannot see your profile, send you messages, or appear in your feed.

Community guidelines in short

  • ✅ Share honestly. Be vulnerable. Express yourself.
  • ✅ Support others. Leave kind reactions. Send compliments.
  • ❌ Don't harass, bully, or target anyone.
  • ❌ Don't share anyone's personal information.
  • ❌ Don't post explicit or violent content.
  • ❌ Don't use this platform to harm yourself or others.

Contact safety team

For urgent safety concerns, email safety@annomuss.in. We take every message seriously.
